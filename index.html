About

Rick Adams

Managing Director, Fir Solutions

Rick leads Fir Solutions, the operations and business development engine behind The Fir Group family of companies. Based in the Boise/Meridian area, he focuses on the systems, partnerships, and market strategy that let law firms grow without losing focus on the practice of law.

His approach is relationship-first and results-driven: build the right structure at the right time, test alignment before formalizing it, and invest in the people who make growth possible. That philosophy shapes every engagement — practical solutions sized to where a firm actually is, not where a generic playbook says it should be.
